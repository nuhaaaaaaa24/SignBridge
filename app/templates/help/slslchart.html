Source: Department of Social Services, Sri Lanka. (2025, September 23). In Introduction to the Sign Language of Sri Lanka (p. 21).

About the Chart

This chart displays the Sri Lankan Sign Language fingerspelling alphabet. Each letter corresponds to a specific hand gesture used to spell out words letter by letter.

Things to know about Sign Language

  • Pay attention to the shape, movement, and position of your hand.
  • Use your dominant hand for single-handed letters.
  • Keep your body relaxed while signing to ensure smooth and fluid movements.
  • Use a mirror to check your hand shapes.
